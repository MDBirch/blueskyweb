Methods, Skills and Resources Needed to Complete Digital Projects Successfully


(1.2)


Most reasonably sized digital projects today are group collaborations of multiple team members working on different areas of the project. Project managers can be considered a critical role in successfully completing digital projects. "Today's project managers are moving their teams and processes online to keep up with the competition and deliver at a faster pace than ever before. Using online project management, collaboration software and cloud file storage systems, work is getting done efficiently and with better results"(reference). The kind of skills required by a manager include: strong communication skills, problem solving, attention to detail, organisational skills, ability to prioritize, ability to delegate effectively and be tech savvy. Also the correct team members have to be selected for their ability to fulfill their role in the process, otherwise this can lead to delay and problems. The kind of resources used in today's projects are increasingly cloud based project management tools, in the form of gantt charts or kanban style planning such as Trello, so that team members review the project stages and can also download and share files quickly and effectively. Communication tools like Slack keeps work feedback and communication referenced in one location for the whole team to review when needed.
