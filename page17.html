Testing of Digital Solutions

3.4 A lot of testing throughout the whole process leads to a better finished product. As the website was constructed stage by stage, the webpages would be tested seprately in their HTML formats, modified and improved as seen fit. Then as layers of CSS were added to create the aesthetics, they would continually be saved, displayed, modified and saved, until the desired effects had been accomplished. Testing of the finished product on different web browsers and different devices to make sure the website displayed itself correctly on each platform is also undertaken.
