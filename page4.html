Plan and Carry out Tasks Using IT

In the making of this website, planning had to be undertaken before and during the project. Online resources have made workflow management much more effective. Kanban style online applications such as Trello allow you to set out tasks and see what stage they are at in the process, in a visual, easy to understand and organised way. In group based projects, multiple team members can add to the Trello board, and update the rest of the team on their progress on certain tasks, or parts of the process. You can click this link to view the Trello board used by myself for this website. You can see that the tasks were set out in order of what needed to be done before the next part of the process could be completed. The tasks start off in the 'to do' phase, then are moved over to the 'doing' phase once work has commenced on them. Eventually once completed they are moved to the 'done' section of the board. At a glance, the user can quickly see what stage the project is at, and keep themselves on track.
