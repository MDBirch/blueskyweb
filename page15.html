Ways to Improve Productiivity and Efficiency


(3.2)


In terms of a team project, if all team members are not in the same location, Zoom meetings would have to be held, which also adds a bit more time and is not as productive as being togther and talking in person. Also file sharing through Slack, to check over completed work and returning with suggestions is slower than discussions in person looking at the same screen. I believe working in a team could handle a larger workload and produce a higher quality final product if all members were at the same loaction. For this improved productivity and efficiency to happen, would require a capable team co-ordinater to oversee the whole project who can keep the process on track, and to delegate tasks to the appropriate person.
