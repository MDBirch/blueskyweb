Review of Benefits and Drawbacks of IT Tools and Systems used

3.1 This project was conducted independently, but productivity could have been boosted by working in a team. Despite this, working alone was enjoyable, and was quite efficient in terms of time saved by not having to co-ordinate a project of multiple team members, with stand up meetings and regular updating of what stage each person was at. By working independently, the coding of HTML with CSS side by side, has the benefit of being customised to a singular viewpoint, but admittedly is probably more time consuming than if done by seperate people. Trello was definately a contributing factor to improved efficiency and productivity, by conducting a project in stages, and in the correct order it limited problems later on in the process. Comparing working in a team to working alone and the impact it would have on the overall time saved and final quality of the project is hard to quantify.
