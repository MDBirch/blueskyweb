Content and Layout of Website


(1.1)


In terms of layout, an important aim of the website was to give the user a clear way to navigate to the various sections and pages. At the top of each page is the navbar. Users can click on 'Home' to return to the front page (index page), then each section has it's seperate button on the navbar, such as 'Unit 1 Outcome 1', 'Unit 1 Outcome 2', 'Unit 1 Outcome 3', 'Unit 2 Outcome 1', 'Unit 2 Outcome 3' and the 'About me' button. For the Unit buttons, the user can hover their pointer over these, and a dropdown menu will display with all the subsections within that Unit and a references page.
The home page contains a short introduction to the website, what it is about and what kind of information is available in the different sections. Each subsection of the 'Units' has it's own page, with a heading and the main body of text. The 'about me' section contains a bit of information on myself, my experience and how to contact me.
