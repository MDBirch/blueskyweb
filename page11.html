Strengths and Weaknesses of Final Project


(2.3)


The website is fully functional and easy to navigate. The final product is slightly lacking in aesthetics. Better looking banners, logos and images could have been designed and implemeneted, and give a more professional appearance, however the quality of these had to be sacrificed due to time constraints. If the project was done again, more time could have been put in to the design side of the project, in terms of better use of CSS to add more features to the website.
