Solutions to Improve own Productivity using IT in Digital Projects


(3.3)


As stated previously, a team could handle a larger workload. If this project were to be undertaken again in a group, good team work would be a critical factor in the project's success. To bring this about would need a good team leader to set out the project clearly and logically. Subsequently, good communication between seperate team members would bring clarity improve the quality of work. An IT system such as Slack would be beneficial for this as all conversations are logged, and can be reviewed if team members go astray. Trello would also provide a clear visual to the whole team, as to where the rest of the team are up to, and what areas they are currently working on.
